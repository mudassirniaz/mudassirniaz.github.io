Messages

Punjab Daanish Schools and Centres of Excellence Authority is a platform for educating and grooming children in remote and under developed areas of Punjab by providing quality education. The Government has provided the required financial and administrative support while seeking improvements in the existing system to make the Authority economically viable and financially sustainable, while increasing the number of beneficiaries.
I am satisfied with the efforts made by Minister School Education Department, Vice Chairperson of the Authority, Secretary School Education Department, Officials and Staff of PDS & CE Authority in achieving and implementing the objectives set out by the Government to steer the Authority in new direction.

Chaudhry Parvez Elahi
Chief Minister Punjab

My advice to the management and faculty is to continue their relentless work with even more commitment and vigor to ensure everlasting success. I am confident and hopeful that the steady growth in the right direction shall enable the Authority to achieve the required aims and objectives.

PDS&CE Authority is providing quality education purely on merit to students all across the province. Daanish Schools offer residential schooling with a focus on deserving category, while Centres of Excellence cater to day schooling for all segments of society. Important decisions pertaining to Rationalization of Staff, Induction of Day Scholars in Daanish Schools and adoption of Austerity Measures have been successfully implemented. About Us
Punjab Daanish Schools and Centres of Excellence Authority (PDS&CEA) Act – February 24, 2010 paved way for the establishment of Punjab Daanish Schools and Centres of Excellence Authority to provide quality Elementary, Secondary and Higher Secondary Education to under privileged and other segments of society. The Authority operates and manages a unique combination of Boarding Schools ( Daanish) and Day Schools ( Centres of Excellence). The schools are spread all across Punjab to cater for quality education for students belonging to the most marginalized and other sections of the society, focusing upon upward social mobility and literacy across the Province.
