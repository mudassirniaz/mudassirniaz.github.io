Daaniah School Boys Chishitian

Daanish School Boys Chishtian is one of the best Boarding Government institutions. This institution provides the basic needs of those students who do not fulfill their educational requirements. It was established in 2010. The facilities provided to the student are a hostel, mess, utility items, sports grounds, IT lab, and Library. Every year an entry test is held for the selection of students.
